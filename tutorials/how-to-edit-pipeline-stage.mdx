## Step by step procedure

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to &#x22;Dashboard&#x22;">
    ![](/images/tutorials/dashboard2-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click your &#x22;company name&#x22;">
    ![](/images/tutorials/dashboard1c-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click &#x22;Management > Pipelines&#x22; from the sidebar.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/pipelines-select1-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the &#x22;category&#x22;. e.g. Client/Personnel/Assignments">
    ![](/images/tutorials/pipelines-select2-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the &#x22;menu icon&#x22; beside the pipeline stage. A pop-up will appear.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/pipelines-edit1-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill up the form, then hit &#x22;Save&#x22;.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/pipelines-edit2-e.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
