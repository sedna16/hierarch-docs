<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to &#x22;Dashboard&#x22;">

    ![](/images/tutorials/personnel-edit1-e.png)
  </Step>

    ![](/images/tutorials/personnel-change-role1-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select a different role.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/personnel-change-role2-e.png)

  <Step title="Then hit &#x22;Save&#x22;.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/personnel-change-role3-e.png)
