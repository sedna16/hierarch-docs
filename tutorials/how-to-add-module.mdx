## Step by step procedure

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your workflow.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/workflow_open_workflow.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the plus icon below the module or trigger.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/workflow_click_plus_icon_module.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the empty module.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/workflow_empty_module.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select your desired module.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/workflow_select_module.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the newly added module. Then add your data.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/workflow_click_new_module.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Then click &#x22;Save&#x22;.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/workflow_save_button.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
