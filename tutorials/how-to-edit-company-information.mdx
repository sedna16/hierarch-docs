## Step by step procedure

1\. Go to **Dashboard**

![](/images/tutorials/dashboard1-e.png)

2\. Click your **company name**

![](/images/tutorials/dashboard-company-name-e.png)

3\. Then click **settings** from the menu bar above the company name & logo.

![](/images/tutorials/company-settings1-e.png)

4\. Fill up the **fields** and it will automatically update.

![](/images/tutorials/company-settings2-e.png)
