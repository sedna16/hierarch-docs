---
title: "How to delete your company?"
---

1\. Go to **Dashboard**

![](/images/tutorials/dashboard2-e.png)

2\. Click your **company name**

![](/images/tutorials/dashboard-company-name-e.png)

3\. Then click **delete** from the menu bar above the company name & logo.

![](/images/tutorials/company-delete1-e.png)

4\. Lastly, click **Delete Anyway**.

![](/images/tutorials/company-delete2-e.png)
