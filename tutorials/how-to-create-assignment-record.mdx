## Step by step procedure

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to &#x22;Dashboard&#x22;">
    ![](/images/tutorials/dashboard2-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click your &#x22;company name&#x22;">
    ![](/images/tutorials/dashboard1c-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click &#x22;Sales > Assignments&#x22; from the sidebar.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/assignments-selections1-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the &#x22;Add&#x22; button above the kanban board.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/assignments-create1-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill up the field forms.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/assignments-create2-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign &#x22;Handbooks&#x22; by clicking the add link. A pop-up with a selection of handbooks. Then select from there.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/assignments-create3-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign &#x22;Clients&#x22; by clicking the add link. A pop-up with a selection of client names. Then select from there.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/assignments-create4-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Assign &#x22;Personnel&#x22; by clicking the add link. A pop-up with a selection of personnel. Then select from there.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/assignments-create5-e.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Then hit &#x22;Assign&#x22; from the right sidebar.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/assignments-create6-e.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
