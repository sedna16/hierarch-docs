## Workflows

A workflow is a sequence of interconnected tasks or activities within a business process, defining how work is organized and executed. It outlines the steps, dependencies, and conditions necessary to complete a specific goal or project. Workflow automation tools are often employed to streamline and optimize these processes, improving efficiency and reducing manual intervention.
