---
title: "Templates"
---

Form templates help your agency move faster by providing ready-made structures for common tasks like client onboarding, project briefs, feedback collection, and approvals. Instead of building forms from scratch, you can use pre-designed templates to standardize processes, ensuring consistency across your team. This not only saves time but also reduces errors and improves client interactions by making it easy to collect the right information upfront.
