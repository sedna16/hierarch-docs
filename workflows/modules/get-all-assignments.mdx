## Overview

The "Get All Assignments" module allows you to retrieve multiple assignments at once, making it easy to view and manage your workload. You can also organize the results by ordering them based on specific column names, ensuring you can quickly find what you need. This feature streamlines assignment retrieval for better efficiency.
