## Overview

The "Get All Inquiry" module allows you to retrieve all inquiry records in your pipeline, providing a complete overview of potential client or lead inquiries. This feature makes it easy to stay organized by tracking all incoming inquiries in one place. It ensures you never miss an opportunity to engage or follow up.
