## Overview

The "Assignment Edited" trigger initiates a workflow when an assignment in the pipeline is modified or updated. This trigger activates at the start of the workflow, allowing you to automate actions or processes in response to changes made to assignments. By utilizing this trigger, you can streamline your workflow and ensure that relevant tasks are executed promptly whenever an assignment is edited.
