---
title: 'Payments'
---

Handling payments and subscriptions is seamless, giving you full control over billing without the hassle. Whether you need to process one-time transactions or set up recurring payments, the system makes it easy to manage everything in one place. With built-in support for Stripe and PayPal, you can securely accept payments from customers worldwide. Automated invoicing and real-time tracking help streamline cash flow, while seamless integration with your workflows ensures a smooth payment experience. Plus, you can connect payments with your existing automations, triggering actions like confirmation emails, reminders, or access changes based on successful transactions.
