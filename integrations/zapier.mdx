## How to setup connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your Zapier account" />

  <Step title="Then navigate to your Zaps - https://zapier.com/app/zaps">
    ![](/images/integrations/zapier001.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Create button">
    ![](/images/integrations/zapier002.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click New Zap">
    ![](/images/integrations/zapier003.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click 1.Trigger">
    ![](/images/integrations/zapier004.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Then search for Webhooks, make sure you have a paid account">
    Then click on that

    ![](/images/integrations/zapier005.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Set the Event to Catch Hook">
    ![](/images/integrations/zapier006.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Continue twice">
    ![](/images/integrations/zapier007.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Then on the Test tab, click the Copy button to copy the webhook URL">
    Then click the Continue button below

    ![](/images/integrations/zapier008.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Then paste the webhook URL to your integrations panel">
    Tick the Connected to App checkbox

    ![](/images/integrations/zapier009.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="On the Actions tab, tick the checkboxes that you want.">
    Then hit Save

    ![](/images/integrations/zapier010.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
