## How to setup connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Go to https://api.slack.com/apps (Login required)" />

  <Step title="Create an App">
    ![](/images/integrations/slack001.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click &#x22;From scratch&#x22;">
    ![](/images/integrations/slack002.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Fill up the details, then click Create App.">
    ![](/images/tutorials/slack003.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Incoming Webhooks">
    ![](/images/integrations/slack004.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Activate Incoming Webhooks">
    ![](/images/integrations/slack005.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Add New Webhook to Workspace">
    ![](/images/integrations/slack006.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Select the channel for the webhook, then click Allow">
    FYI: You will need to create 3 webhooks for Assignments, Clients and Personnel

    ![](/images/integrations/slack007.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Then copy the webhook url to your integration panel.">
    Tick the **Connected to App** checkbox

    ![](/images/integrations/slack008.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the checkboxes on the Actions tab">
    Then hit **Save**

    ![](/images/integrations/slack009.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
