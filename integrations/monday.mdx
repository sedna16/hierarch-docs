## How to setup connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your Monday.com account" />

  <Step title="Click on your profile icon on the top right corner of the screen">
    ![](/images/integrations/monday001.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Developers">
    ![](/images/integrations/monday002.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click My access tokens on the sidebar">
    ![](/images/integrations/monday003.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Show">
    ![](/images/integrations/monday004.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Then click Copy">
    ![](/images/integrations/monday005.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to your integrations panel and paste the API token">
    Click the Connected to App checkbox

    ![](/images/integrations/monday006.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go back to monday.com">
    Click on your workspace

    ![](/images/integrations/monday007.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy workspace id from the url">
    ![](/images/integrations/monday008.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on your board">
    ![](/images/integrations/monday009.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy your board id from the url">
    ![](/images/integrations/monday010.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste these on the Settings tab on the integrations panel">
    ![](/images/integrations/monday011.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Actions tab, click the checkbox that you want">
    Then hit Save

    /image
  </Step>
</Steps>
