## How to setup connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your Discord account on your browser" />

  <Step title="Click the gear icon beside the channel name">
    ![](/images/integrations/discord001.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Integrations">
    ![](/images/integrations/discord002.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Webhooks">
    ![](/images/integrations/discord003.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click New Webhook button">
    ![](/images/integrations/discord004.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on the new Webhook created">
    ![](/images/integrations/discord005.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Copy Webhook URL button">
    ![](/images/integrations/discord006.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste the webhook on the integrations panel">
    Tick the **Connected to App** checkbox

    ![](/images/integrations/discord007.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Exit the Discord settings menu" />

  <Step title="Copy the Guild ID from the URL">
    ![](/images/integrations/discord009.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Then paste the Guild ID to the field">
    ![](/images/integrations/discord011.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on your channel">
    Then copy the **channel id** from the URL

    ![](/images/integrations/discord010.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste the Channel ID(s) to the fields">
    ![](/images/integrations/discord011.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to the Actions tab">
    Then click the checkboxes that you want

    Hit **Save**

    ![](/images/integrations/discord012.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
