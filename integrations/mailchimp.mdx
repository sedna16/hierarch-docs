## How to setup connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your Mailchimp account">
    ![](/images/integrations/mailchimp001.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to this url - https://admin.mailchimp.com/account/api">
    And click Create A Key

    ![](/images/integrations/mailchimp002.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Give it a name">
    And click Generate Key

    /image
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy your Key">
    And click Done
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go back to your mailchimp account">
    And copy your server prefix from the url
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste your personal access token on the integrations panel">
    Click Connected to App
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go back to your mailchimp account">
    Click Audience dashboard from the sidebar
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Manage Audience">
    Then View audiences from the dropdown
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the dropdown icon beside Stats">
    Then Settings
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Audience name and campaign defaults" />

  <Step title="Copy your list ID or audience ID" />

  <Step title="Paste these info on the Settings tab" />

  <Step title="Go to Actions tab">
    And click the checkboxes you want
    Then hit Save
  </Step>
</Steps>
