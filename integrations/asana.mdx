## How to setup connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your Asana account" />

  <Step title="Click on your Profile icon on the top right corner">
    ![](/images/integrations/asana001.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Then click Settings">
    ![](/images/integrations/asana002.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click &#x22;Apps&#x22;">
    ![](/images/integrations/asana003.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click &#x22;Manage Developer Apps&#x22;">
    ![](/images/integrations/asana004.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click &#x22;Create new token&#x22;">
    ![](/images/integrations/asana005.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Give it a name, tick the checkbox">
    Then click Create token
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Copy" />

  <Step title="Paste the Access token on the integration panel" />

  <Step title="Go to https://app.asana.com/api/1.0/workspaces">
    Then copy the "gid" of your workspace
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go back to https://app.asana.com/0/home/">
    Click one of your projects
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy your channel id from the url" />

  <Step title="Paste these on the integration panel, Settings tab" />

  <Step title="Go to Actions tab">
    Click the checkboxes that you want
    Then hit Save
  </Step>
</Steps>
