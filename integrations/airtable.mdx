## How to setup connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your Airtable Account" />

  <Step title="Create your Workspace">
    Then create your first Base

    ![](/images/integrations/airtable001.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on your profile on the top right corner">
    Then click Developer hub

    ![](/images/integrations/airtable002.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Personal access tokens">
    Then click Create token

    ![](/images/integrations/airtable003.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Give it a name">
    Add Scopes - **data.records.read** and **data.records.write**
    Add a Base
    Then hit Create Token

    ![](/images/integrations/airtable004.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy your personal access token">
    And hit Done

    ![](/images/integrations/airtable005.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste your personal access token on the integrations panel">
    Click Connected to App

    ![](/images/integrations/airtable006.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go back to Airtable">
    Click Back to Home

    ![](/images/integrations/airtable007.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click on your Base">
    ![](/images/integrations/airtable008.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy the Base ID from the url">
    ![](/images/integrations/airtable009.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy the table ID from the url">
    ![](/images/integrations/airtable010.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste these info on the Settings tab on the integrations panel">
    ![](/images/integrations/airtable011.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Actions tab">
    And click the checkboxes you want
    Then hit Save

    ![](/images/integrations/airtable012.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
