## How to setup connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your Hubspot account - https://app.hubspot.com/login/" />

  <Step title="Click the gear icon (Settings) on the upper right corner">
    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot001.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Integrations dropdown on the sidebar">
    Then click Private Apps

    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot002.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click &#x22;Create a private app&#x22;">
    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot003.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Give it a name, then click the Scopes tab">
    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot004.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the CRM dropdown, then click all checkboxes">
    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot005.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the Standard dropdown, then click the &#x22;tickets&#x22; checkbox">
    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot006.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Then click the Create App button on the upper right corner">
    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot007.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Copy on the pop-up, hit Close">
    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot008.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Then click the Auth tab">
    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot009.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the &#x22;Copy&#x22; link from the Client secret section">
    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot010.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste these on the integrations panel Click the Connected to App checkbox">
    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot011.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go back to Hubspot and copy your company ID from the URL">
    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot012.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="And paste it on the Settings tab for the integrations panel">
    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot013.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to the Actions tab">
    Tick the checkboxes that you want

    Then hit Save

    ![](/images/integrations/hubspot014.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
