## How to setup connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your ActiveCampaign Account" />

  <Step title="Click Settings on the sidebar" />

  <Step title="Click Developer on the sidebar" />

  <Step title="Click Copy API Url">
    Then click Copy API Key
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste these info on the integrations panel">
    Click Connected to App
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go back to ActiveCampaign">
    And click Contacts from the sidebar
    Then click on your List
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy your list ID from the url" />

  <Step title="And paste it on the Settings tab" />

  <Step title="Go to Actions tab">
    And click the checkboxes you want
    Then hit Save
  </Step>
</Steps>
