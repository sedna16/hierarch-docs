## How to setup connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your make.com account" />

  <Step title="Click Scenarios on the sidebar">
    ![](/images/integrations/make001.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Hit Create a new scenario">
    ![](/images/integrations/make002.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click the Plus icon to add a module">
    ![](/images/integrations/make003.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Type in the search box &#x22;webhook&#x22;">
    Then click the Webhooks menu

    ![](/images/integrations/make004.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Custom webhook">
    ![](/images/integrations/make005.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Custom webhook">
    ![](/images/integrations/make006.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Give it a name, then hit Save">
    ![](/images/integrations/make007.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Copy address to clipboard">
    ![](/images/integrations/make008.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Then paste it on the integrations panel">
    And click Connected to App checkbox

    ![](/images/integrations/make009.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="On the Actions tab, tick the checkboxes that you want.">
    And hit Save

    ![](/images/integrations/make010.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Then go back to your scenario on Make">
    Add modules to it

    And turn it ON

    ![](/images/integrations/make011.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
