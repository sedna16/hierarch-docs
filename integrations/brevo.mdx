## How to setup connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your Brevo Account" />

  <Step title="Click on your profile on the top right corner">
    Then click SMTP & API
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click API Keys tab" />

  <Step title="Click Generate a new API key" />

  <Step title="Give it a name, then click Generate" />

  <Step title="Copy your api key, then click OK" />

  <Step title="Go to your integrations panel and paste the API token">
    Click the Connected to App checkbox
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Contacts">
    Then click Lists
  </Step>

  <Step title="Copy your list ID" />

  <Step title="Paste it on the Settings tab" />

  <Step title="Go to Actions tab">
    And click the checkboxes you want
    Then hit Save
  </Step>
</Steps>
