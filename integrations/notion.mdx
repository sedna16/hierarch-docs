## How to setup connection

<Steps>
  <Step title="Login to your Notion Account" />

  <Step title="Click on your workspace">
    And click on your page

    ![](/images/integrations/notion001.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click this icon on the top right corner">
    ![](/images/integrations/notion002.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Hover Connect to">
    And click Manage Connections

    ![](/images/integrations/notion003.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="A new tab will open, click Develop or manage connections">
    ![](/images/integrations/notion004.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click New Integration">
    ![](/images/integrations/notion005.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Give it a name, then Submit">
    ![](/images/integrations/notion006.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Click Show, then Copy">
    ![](/images/integrations/notion007.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go back to your main page and click this icon again">
    ![](/images/integrations/notion002.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Hover Connect to again">
    Then click your api

    ![](/images/integrations/notion008.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste your API key on the integrations panel">
    Click Connected to App

    ![](/images/integrations/notion009.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go back to your workspace">
    Click your Database

    ![](/images/integrations/notion010.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Get the database ID from the url">
    ![](/images/integrations/notion011.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Paste it on the Settings tab on the integrations panel">
    ![](/images/integrations/notion012.png)
  </Step>

  <Step title="Go to Actions tab">
    And click the checkboxes you want
    Then hit Save

    ![](/images/integrations/notion013.png)
  </Step>
</Steps>
